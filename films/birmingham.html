A psychogeographical escapade into the sensory capabilities of Birmingham, my hometown. Leopold Bloom accompanies my homecoming, showing me the vivid pleasure of the present, rescuing me from the messy ruins of memory, retrieving the city from the quicksand of emotional longing.
